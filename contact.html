Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to start retirement planning?

The best time is now! Whether you're 10 years or 2 years from retirement, starting to research and plan will give you more options and time to prepare.

Do I need to speak the local language?

Many popular retirement destinations have large expat communities where English is widely spoken. However, learning basic phrases is always helpful and appreciated.

How much money do I need to retire abroad?

It depends on your lifestyle and chosen destination. Many retirees live comfortably on $1,500-$2,500 per month in affordable destinations. Our guides provide detailed breakdowns.

Is healthcare affordable in these countries?

Yes! Many popular retirement destinations offer quality healthcare at a fraction of US costs. We provide detailed healthcare information for each destination.

How long does visa processing typically take?

Timeline varies by country, typically ranging from 2 weeks to 3 months. Our visa guides include specific processing times for each destination.

Will my Social Security/pension work abroad?

Yes, most pensions and Social Security payments continue abroad. However, there are tax implications - consult a tax professional for specifics.
